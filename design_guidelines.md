# Design Guidelines for Longevity Startup Platform

## Design Approach
**Reference-Based Approach** - Drawing inspiration from modern health and wellness platforms like Headspace, Calm, and Oura, combined with the clean, trustworthy aesthetic of medical/health startups. The design should convey scientific credibility while remaining approachable and inspiring.

## Core Design Elements

### A. Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Deep Teal: 180 65% 25% (trust, health, longevity)
- Soft Green: 150 40% 45% (vitality, growth)

**Supporting Colors:**
- Warm White: 45 15% 97% (clean, medical)
- Light Gray: 210 8% 94% (subtle backgrounds)
- Dark Charcoal: 220 15% 15% (text, dark mode)

**Accent Colors (minimal use):**
- Coral: 15 75% 65% (calls-to-action, highlights)

**Gradients:**
- Hero backgrounds: Subtle teal to soft green gradients
- Section dividers: Light gray to warm white
- Card overlays: Semi-transparent teal gradients

### B. Typography
**Primary Font:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headers: 600-700 weight
- Body: 400-500 weight
- Captions: 400 weight

**Secondary Font:** Merriweather (for editorial content)
- Article headings and featured quotes

### C. Layout System
**Tailwind Spacing Units:** Consistent use of 4, 8, 12, and 16 units
- Component padding: p-8, p-12
- Section margins: m-16
- Element spacing: gap-4, gap-8

### D. Component Library
**Navigation:** Clean header with logo, minimal menu items, prominent CTA button
**Hero Section:** Large background image with overlay, compelling headline, and clear value proposition
**Resource Cards:** Clean cards with subtle shadows, hover effects, and clear typography
**Forms:** Rounded inputs with soft shadows, focus states in primary teal
**Buttons:** Primary (teal), secondary (outline), with subtle hover animations
**Calculator Interface:** Step-by-step form with progress indicators
**Blog Cards:** Image thumbnails, excerpt text, read-more links

### E. Visual Treatments
**Backgrounds:** Subtle gradients, clean whites, minimal geometric patterns
**Shadows:** Soft, natural shadows (shadow-sm, shadow-md)
**Border Radius:** Consistent rounded corners (rounded-lg, rounded-xl)
**Animations:** Minimal fade-ins and subtle hover effects only

## Images
**Hero Image:** Large, inspiring image of active older adults or peaceful nature scenes (mountains, ocean) with gradient overlay
**Resource Section:** Lifestyle images showing healthy activities, meditation, exercise
**Team/About:** Professional headshots with consistent styling
**Blog:** Relevant health and wellness stock photography
**Calculator:** Clean iconography representing health metrics

## Page Structure
1. **Hero Section:** Full-height with background image, company mission, primary CTA
2. **Value Proposition:** Three-column layout explaining longevity benefits
3. **Resources Preview:** Card grid showcasing educational content
4. **Calculator Teaser:** Interactive preview of life expectancy tool
5. **Newsletter Signup:** Simple, compelling lead capture form

The overall aesthetic should feel premium yet accessible, scientific yet warm, inspiring confidence in the startup's expertise while making longevity feel attainable for everyone.
